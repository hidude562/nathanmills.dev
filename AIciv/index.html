AIciv

AIciv is a turn-based strategy game where players are able to issue absurd edicts to fix their nation. This program uses GPT-3 for text comprehension, so it can comprehend any input you give it. Running low on food? Just tell the game to make hotdogs an official currency of your nation, and it will adjust the economy of your civilization accordingly. The goal of the game is to have as high of a score as possible by turn 100. You can continue the game afterwards however. You earn score based off of many factors like your military, gdp and other things. It is possible to loose the game before turn 100 if your people revolt or you have a large deficit of money or resources.
