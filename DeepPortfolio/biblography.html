Allado-McDowell, K., and Irenosen Okojie. Pharmako-Ai. Ignota, 2020.

This book was written by K Allado-McDowell and co-written by a GPT-3 model (GPT-3 is a state of the art deep learning model that generates text). The book is a novel that is a dialogue between Allado and GPT-3 where GPT-3 writes procedurally generated chapters of stories and poems, while Allado prompts the AI. This book will be useful for understanding the coherence of the AI and how well deep learning models can perform on complex tasks.


Dunn, Tamara. “Deep Learning.” Salem Press Encyclopedia of Science, Feb. 2022. EBSCOhost,

https://discovery-ebsco-com.proxy.kyvl.org/c/y3ztto/details/2is7nzmt6z

This article covers several topics about deep learning, it includes what deep learning is, the history of deep learning, and an overview of current research on deep learning and how companies are utilizing the technology. This article gives a basic understanding of deep learning and can be used to address to the reader what deep learning is. This article also gives more general information about companies researching deep learning and how they are utilizing it, which can also be used for informing the reader about basic information of deep learning. The publisher, “Salem Press Encyclopedia of Science '', is a reputable source and the article does not contain any known bias.
